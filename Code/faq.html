FAQ

  • 1. what is the vision of Food Sharing Revolution?
    FSR (Food Sharing Revolution) is a Food Waste Management System that bridges the gap between restaurants, functions and events (excess food) and NGOs that run orphanages (deficit food). The excess food is directed to the needy people. This system enables restaurants, event managers, NGOs and logistics to work together in order to use the excess food efficiently.
  • 2. How it Works?
    Food Sharing Revolution, This system enables restaurants, event managers, NGOs and logistics to work together in order to use the excess food efficiently.
  • 3. What is the role of Restaurant?
    The restaurants can upload the details of the excess food and its consumption period.
  • 4. What is the role of NGOs?
    The NGOs can view these details and request the logistics within the feasible distance to pick up and deliver the food.
  • 5. What is role of Logistics?
    The logistics can charge the requesting NGO for deliveries by sending them invoices which is later paid by the NGO.
